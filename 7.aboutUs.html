About Typing Master

Typing Master is dedicated to helping individuals improve their typing skills through comprehensive practice sessions and detailed performance analytics. We believe that strong typing skills are essential in today's digital world.

Features

Real-time Speed Tracking

Monitor your typing speed in words per minute (WPM) and track your live updates.

Precision Accuracy Monitor

Track your typing accuracy percentage and receive feedback to improve your precision.

Detailed Progress History

View comprehensive analytics on your typing performance with personalized insights.
